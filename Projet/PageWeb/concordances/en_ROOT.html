[[225]edit]root
root
Criticisms of ROOT include its difficulty for beginners, as well asroot
various aspects of its design and implementation. Frequent causes ofroot
frustration include extreme [226]code bloat, heavy use of globalroot
variables,^[227][4] and an overcomplicated class hierarchy. From timeroot
to time these issues are discussed on the ROOT users mailingroot
list.^[228][5]^[229][6] While scientists dissatisfied with ROOT have inroot
the past managed to work around its flaws,^[230][7] some of theroot
shortcomings are regularly addressed by the ROOT team. The CINTroot
interpreter, for example, has been replaced by the Clingroot
interpreter,^[231][8] and numerous bugs are fixed with every release.root
