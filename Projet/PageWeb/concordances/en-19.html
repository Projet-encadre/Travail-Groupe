Root Insurance was founded in March 2015 by Alex Timm and Dan Manges.root
root

the company through December 2021. In August 2021, Root announced aroot
partnership deal with [118]Carvana to develop personalized autoroot

The deal, initially formed around a $126 million investment by Carvanaroot

certain stages or tranches. The collaboration uses both companiesroot

a car with Carvana.^[119][4]root

In January 2022, Root closed on a $300 million five-year term loan withroot
BlackRock. The maturity of this term loan is January 27, 2027.^[120][5]root
On March 23, 2023, Root Insurance Chief Revenue and Operating Officer,root
Daniel Rosenthal resigned, having started as a director for Rootroot
Insurance in 2017.^[121][6]root
