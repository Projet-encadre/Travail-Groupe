RootsWeb, acquired by Ancestry in June 2000, is a free genealogyroot
community that uses online forums, mailing lists, and other resourcesroot
to help people research their family history. Users can uploadroot
[300]GEDCOM files of their information for others to search at theroot
WorldConnect portion of the site. Trees uploaded to WorldConnect areroot
searchable at both the RootsWeb and Ancestry Web sites.root
root
On December 20, 2017, a file containing 300,000 RootsWeb user names,root
passwords, and email addresses was exposed to the Internet. The 300,000root
records were from RootsWeb surname list service; 55,000 of thoseroot
records were also Ancestry.com login credentials.^[301][81] By 2024,root
the hosted Web sites were all read-only, disabling all user logins.root
