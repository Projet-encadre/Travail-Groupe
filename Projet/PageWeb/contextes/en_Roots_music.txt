   Roots music may refer to:

     * [51]Americana (music), a style incorporating early blues, country,
       folk, rhythm and blues, and rock influences
     * [52]Folk music
     * [53]Rasin, Haitian roots music
     * [54]Roots reggae
     * [55]Roots revival, a trend which includes young performers
       popularizing the traditional musical styles of their ancestors
     * [56]Roots rock
     * [57]World music
