root for

phrasal verb

   rooted for; rooting for; roots for
   : to express or show support for (a person, a team, etc.) : to hope for
   the success of (someone or something)
   They always root for the home team.
   Good luck on your upcoming show. Remember that we're all rooting for
   you.
